RiseUP Bake #2
"I loafed every minute of the RiseUP event this weekend! From start to finish, it was the perfect recipe for a meaningful and memorable experience.

We got hands-on baking bread (and yes, I kneaded that in my life), learning techniques that made me feel like I could really rise to the occasion in my own kitchen. Our baker, Sean, was absolutely the upper crust: patient, encouraging, and full of wisdom. He truly helped us see that even when life gets tough, we can still rise up (just like a good dough!) and accomplish something amazing.

On top of that, the tour of Urban Peak's 'mothership' was incredibly impactful. Learning about the powerful work they're doing for unhoused youth and young adults in Denver really added depth to the experience—it wasn't just about bread, it was about building a stronger community too.

All in all, this event was the perfect blend of learning, purpose, and inspiration. I came for the bread but stayed for the heart. I'd recommend it to anyone who wants to bake a difference, support a great cause, and have a yeast-ful experience doing it.

I'll definitely be coming back for another batch!" — Eli, RiseUP #2

What to Expect

When should I arrive?
Please plan to arrive at 9:45 AM at the front desk. There is street parking directly in front of the Mothership.

When do we wrap up?
We'll finish just before 1:00 PM.

Is there a break?
Yes — we'll enjoy coffee and pastries together during the second rise.

Is there any paperwork?
You'll be asked to fill out a short Volunteer form when you arrive.

Photography policy
Out of respect for the privacy of the youth at Urban Peak, please refrain from taking photos of them.

Do I need baking experience?
Not at all — if you've never baked bread before, that's completely fine. We'll learn together.

Do I need to bring an apron?
Aprons are provided for everyone. You're welcome to bring your own if you prefer.

What should I bring?
All ingredients are provided — just bring yourself!

Can I take my loaf home?
Yes! Your loaf is yours to take home and enjoy.
